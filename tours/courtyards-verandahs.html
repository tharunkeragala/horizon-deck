Courtyards & Verandahs – Geoffrey Bawa
Architecture Tour in Sri Lanka
Sri Lanka’s most renowned architect Geoffrey Bawa redefined and influenced South-East Asian architecture. This tour is dedicated to his creative genius and you will visit and stay at places he conceptualised or inspired.

Begin your journey with two nights in the cultural triangle at one of Bawa’s iconic hotels, then travel far south for anight up in the mountains of Ella before arriving for a night on the coast of Galle at one of the most iconic of Bawa’s creations.
Why You’ll Love this Geoffrey Bawa Tour Package
  • Embark on a journey through the heart of Sri Lanka, exploring charming courtyards and verandahs that resonate with the island’s rich history, offering an authentic cultural immersion.
  • Indulge in breathtaking landscapes and serene retreats that redefine relaxation. From lush tea plantations to historic estates, each moment promises awe-inspiring beauty and tranquility.
  • Experience the warmth of personalized hospitality at every stop. Our curated itinerary ensures you not only witness the beauty of Sri Lanka but also connect with its people on a personal level.
  • Delight your taste buds with a gastronomic journey. From traditional Sri Lankan delicacies to gourmet experiences, this trip is a feast for those who savor the fusion of flavors.
Is This Trip Right For You?
  • If you crave an authentic cultural experience, this trip is tailored for you. Immerse yourself in the traditions, arts and lifestyles that make Sri Lanka a cultural gem.
  • Ideal for those seeking a balance between exploration and relaxation. While there are enriching activities, the trip allows ample time for you to unwind in the scenic surroundings at your own pace.
  • If capturing moments is your passion, this itinerary unfolds a photographer’s dream. From ancient architecture to vibrant landscapes, every frame tells a story of Sri Lanka’s captivating beauty.
  • If your travel style involves staying in distinctive accommodations, from historic mansions to boutique hotels, this journey promises a blend of comfort and uniqueness that complements your taste.

Sigiriya Rock

Climb up to admire the frescoes on the rock face. Then reach the summit where 360 degree views, and timeless ruins await you.

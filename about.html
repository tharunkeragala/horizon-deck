Welcome to HorizonDeck
Your Home of Legendary Sri Lankan Hospitality
A part of the HorizonDeck family, our journey began in 1980 to show visitors from near and far, the wonders of the paradise we call home. Today, we are a leading destination management company renowned for our legendary hospitality. Our team comprises experienced professionals who know the country and take pride in sharing its treasures. Our mission is to ensure that every guest enjoys the best experience possible while in our care. The many global accolades we have received confirm our deep commitment to excellence in all we do and our ability to deliver the highest of service standards.
  • We place a premium on providing a “legendary” service
  • We have a well maintained fleet of vehicles and source the best available when required
  • We perform to the highest standards possible and always strive to improve
  • We pride ourselves on reliability
  • We have over 50 years of experience and counting
  • We provide excellent value for money


Founder Chairman, HorizonDeck
Tharun Keragala
In the early 1970s, amidst the rhythmic dance of waves along Negombo beach, Herbert Cooray stood, a dreamer captivated by the brightening horizon. Founder extraordinaire of Jetwing, he birthed the intimate haven, Blue Oceanic. This humble abode of six rooms whispered warmth, marking the genesis of our pioneering journey.

Jetwing Travels, a familial tapestry woven with passion, humility, integrity and tenacity, has evolved into Sri Lanka's tourism maestro. For over four decades, we've sculpted an unrivaled hospitality culture, embracing innovation, sustainability and community upliftment. Join us on a journey where you transcend being a mere visitor—you become family.
